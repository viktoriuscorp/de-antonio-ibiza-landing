DE ANTONIO YACHTS D60: THE NEW FLAGSHIP WITH OFFICIAL DISTRIBUTION IN IBIZA AND FORMENTERA

De Antonio Yachts Ibiza, the official distributor of the prestigious brand in Ibiza and Formentera, proudly announces the arrival of the spectacular D60, marking a new milestone in the range of yachts over 18 meters in length. This model, designed to redefine luxury and innovation, will be exclusively available in the islands starting summer 2025.

A Step Forward in Performance and Efficiency

As the flagship of De Antonio Yachts, the D60 combines years of expertise and development while maintaining the brand's signature design language. Equipped with four hidden Mercury V12 600 hp outboard engines, it delivers a top speed exceeding 45 knots and an efficient cruising speed of 30 knots. This innovative hidden outboard engine solution ensures a sleek aesthetic while merging the best of inboard and outboard architectures for unparalleled performance and efficiency.

Outdoor Spaces That Maximize Comfort

The D60 design makes the most of every inch, offering an unmatched experience on deck with its walk-around layout that integrates three distinct areas:

Aft area: A chill-out solarium with a central table and integrated ice bucket.

Central dining zone: Spacious and sun-protected, accommodating more than 10 guests with adaptable features for any occasion.

Bow area: Multiple configuration options, including a main solarium, large sofas, versatile tables, and even a hidden jacuzzi revealed by sliding the solarium cover.

    The hardtop roof adds further flexibility, with the option of full glazing for perfect climate control or opening through large side windows and sliding rear doors. These features create a versatile, adaptable space ideal for enjoying the stunning surroundings of Ibiza and Formentera.

    Interiors Designed for Luxury and Flexibility

    The D60 also stands out for its innovative interior layout, including:

    A mid-level galley, seamlessly integrated or enclosed with sliding doors for added flexibility.

    A loft-style central distributor that connects interior spaces and leads to a generous master suite featuring a private bathroom, walk-in closet, sofa, and desk.

    Two bow cabin configurations: either two separate cabins or one large cabin, complemented by a courtesy bathroom for guests.

    A crew cabin with independent access, two bunks, and a private bathroom.

      State-of-the-Art Design and Manufacturing

      Built using infusion lamination and featuring an "all-round" hull design, the D60 is engineered for efficient long-distance cruising as well as agile, sporty coastal navigation. Combining cutting-edge aesthetics, functionality, and customization, it offers a unique boating experience, perfect for exploring the pristine waters of Ibiza and Formentera.

      Exclusively in Ibiza and Formentera with De Antonio Yachts Ibiza

      As the official distributor, De Antonio Yachts Ibiza ensures that sailing enthusiasts in the Pitiusas have exclusive access to the extraordinary D60, a masterpiece of design, performance, and luxury.

      The first units will be available in summer 2025. Discover the unparalleled pleasure of sailing aboard the new D60 with us!
